How to Pick an Outfit
Step by Step Guide
STEP 1 Pick your VIBE (this is your main character energy)
What are you going for today?
  • Rich CEO in disguise
  • Silent luxury, don't talk to me
  • Main character at a rooftop party
  • Soft glam but still expensive
  • Dangerously well-dressed
STEP 2 Choose your BASE outfit
This is your foundation—no chaos yet. Pick one:
  • Sharp trousers + fitted top
  • Bodycon dress / sleek midi dress
  • Oversized blazer + shorts or mini skirt
  • Clean co-ord set (matching top + bottom)
  • Simple fitted jeans + elevated top
STEP 3 Add the "I have taste" layer
Now we upgrade it:
  • Blazer (instant power boost)
  • Trench coat (mysterious + expensive vibes)
  • Knit cardigan (soft luxury)
  • Leather jacket (main character energy unlocked)
STEP 4 Choose your color mood
Don't overthink—just pick:
  • Black & white (timeless elite)
  • Neutrals (quiet luxury)
  • Deep tones (rich energy)
  • One pop color (for drama)
STEP 5 Shoes = personality reveal
What are you stepping out in?
  • Heels (power walk mode activated)
  • Loafers (rich student energy)
  • Clean sneakers (effortless cool)
  • Boots (don't mess with me vibe)
STEP 6 Accessories (aka final boss items)
Pick wisely—don't overstack:
  • Small bag or clutch
  • Watch or bracelet
  • Earrings or chain
  • Sunglasses (instant upgrade)

FINAL STEP: The cheat code Put it on like you already own the place.
If it feels slightly "too much," you're probably doing it right.
